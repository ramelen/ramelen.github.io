Ramble (from middle english ramelen)

Noun

A leisurely stroll.
an instance of someone talking at length without direction.
A section of woodland suitable for leisurely walking.

Verb

To move about aimlessly.
To walk for pleasure.
To talk or write incessantly, unclearly, or incoherently, with many digressions.
To follow a winding path or course.

I don't quite know how to describe myself. I've always thought of myself as a very weird person, but the more I learn about people, the ordinary I realise I am. I'm certainly not the poster child of mundane, per say, but I wouldn't say I'm anything extrordinary.

There's the superficial description of me; I was born in Calgary and native to the Okanagan Valley of British Columbia, Canada; I've got a trans brother and my paternal grandmother is a Korean immigrant; I've never traveled much, but I spent much of my teenage years at a very international school.

Then there's the cover letter description of me; I'm in high school, looking to do a BSc of Computer Science at UBCO; I'm intellectually gifted and seeking an ADHD diagnosis (My brother and father have it as well); I'm great at critical thinking and picking things up quickly, and I always get the job done.

I've got a creative side too; I love math and programming and languages; I've written 10000 words for a multi-year scavenger hunt; I've designed an entire (small) language with plans for another; I've got a personal website that I use to show off all my stuff, and plans to start developing for AR.

And of course, I've got my insecurities; I'm very stubborn and sometimes inconsiderate of other people; I haven't got much of a social life; I'm underweight and dehydrated a lot of the time (I'm working on this!), and I don't excersise; I'm not very observant, and will often get stuck in my own head thinking about whatever crosses my mind.

But I think the way I ought to describe myself is this: I'm just like everyone else; figuring it out. That's why I call myself Ramelen. My life's just one big ramble, enjoying the sights as I go.
