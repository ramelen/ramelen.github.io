This pallete is sort of the result of a small adventure I had into the world of color theory. All the trouble came from the precision of the RGB encoding. For image processing having 16 million colors is great, but for a minimalistic flat color pallete, 16 million is just way too much choice. How are you supposed to decide what colors are the best?

I'll probably come back to describe my journey, but for now I'll just say that this is a sampling of the OKHSV color space.
